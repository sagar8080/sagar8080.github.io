A 2020 roadmap aimed at someone breaking into data engineering. What to learn, in roughly what order, and which resources I found worth the time. The five sections are programming &amp; data structures, SQL and database internals, big-data systems (Hadoop, Spark, Kafka), data stores (warehouses vs lakes, NoSQL, Delta), and DevOps fundamentals (Linux, CI/CD, containers).

Five years later the resources I&apos;d swap are the streaming chapter (Kafka still good, but I&apos;d add Iceberg + a modern lakehouse) and the storage chapter (DuckDB and Iceberg deserve top billing). The skeleton holds up.
